About Us

Our Founder

Sam started SG Chauffeur with a clear vision: to create a transportation service that makes every journey stress-free, reliable, and comfortable.

With years of experience in the industry, Sam understood the challenges people face when it comes to finding dependable transport for various needs, whether it's a daily commute, a special event, or a cross-border trip.

He wanted to build a company that not only meets those needs but exceeds customer expectations with every ride.

Who We Are

At SG Chauffeur, we are dedicated to providing seamless transportation solutions for everyone.

Based on the belief that travel should be simple and worry-free, we offer a wide range of services to cater to different needs, from airport transfers and VIP rides to school commutes and cross-border travel between Singapore and Malaysia.

Our diverse fleet includes everything from 5-seater sedans to 40-seater buses, ensuring that we have the right vehicle for any occasion.
